## Design Context

### Users
- Primary user is the creator themself, using the product as a personal crypto asset command center.
- They open it to quickly understand net worth, allocations, exchange + wallet balances, and spot patterns through statistics and data analysis.
- The interface should support repeated, daily check-ins where clarity and signal density matter more than social or collaborative workflows.

### Brand Personality
- 3-word personality: refined, calm, analytical.
- Emotional goal: the UI should feel precise, premium, composed, and trustworthy rather than flashy or hype-driven.
- Tone should be concise and mature, with a sense of high-end financial tooling rather than consumer crypto speculation.

### Aesthetic Direction
- Build a premium SaaS interface with a unified dual-theme system (light + dark).
- Visual direction: cold, clean, executive-grade analytics UI with strong typography, disciplined spacing, quiet contrast, and subtle data-terminal cues.
- Prefer elegant restraint over loud gradients or gaming-style “crypto dashboard” tropes.
- Avoid generic AI-SaaS patterns, neon-on-dark palettes, overused glassmorphism, oversized rounded cards, and decorative metric spam.

### Design Principles
1. Surface signal first: make portfolio health, allocation, and trend insight immediately legible.
2. Premium through restraint: use fewer but sharper visual ideas, with deliberate spacing and typography.
3. Analytical calm: interactions and colors should reduce noise and support focused reading.
4. Dual-theme integrity: light and dark modes should both feel intentionally designed, not inverted copies.
5. Local-first trust: the interface should feel private, reliable, and operator-centric.
