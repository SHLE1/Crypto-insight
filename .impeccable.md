## Design Context

### Users
Primary user is the project owner themself: a geek actively tracking personal crypto holdings. The product is used as a private, local-first portfolio dashboard for quickly checking assets across wallets, centralized exchanges, and DeFi positions.

### Brand Personality
The interface should feel: 简洁、明了、干净.

### Aesthetic Direction
A focused dashboard experience optimized for fast scanning rather than spectacle. Prioritize clarity, low visual noise, strong information hierarchy, and quick cross-source comparison. The UI should feel lightweight, trustworthy, and efficient.

### Design Principles
1. Make cross-source portfolio status readable at a glance.
2. Prefer clarity and speed over decorative effects.
3. Keep layouts clean, structured, and low-noise.
4. Help the user identify missing, stale, or incomplete asset data quickly.
5. Preserve a local-first, trustworthy product feel.
