# Q:What are the limits for API requests?

A.

Our free-to-use Starter Plan currently has a limit of 1,000 Compute Units per second (CU/s) and 40,000 CU per day. Over this limit, free accounts are temporarily suspended until the beginning of the next day.

Our Pro Plan has a limit of 1,500 CU/s and 100M CU per month.

Our Business Plan has a limit of 5,000 CU/s and 500M CU per month.

If you go past the the included monthly quotas in Pro or Business, your app will continue to function and all additional requests will be billed according to our overage pricing.

If you need higher limits than the Pro or Business plans offers, feel free to reach out to [success@moralis.io](mailto:success@moralis.io) for an Enterprise or Custom solution.
